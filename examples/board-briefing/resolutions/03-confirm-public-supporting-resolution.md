## Proposed action

That the Board confirm this public example resolution as a demonstration of document-level classification.

## Rationale

The overall package may be `CONFIDENTIAL:BOARD`, while an individual resolution or supporting item may be `PUBLIC`. The viewer will keep the package handling rule on the landing page and show the document's own classification on the detail page.

## Draft resolution text

Resolved, that this example public resolution be included to demonstrate how public material can be presented inside a more restricted briefing package.
